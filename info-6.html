Who holds the funds during the transmission?

All the Money transmitter for the deposits and withdrawals are provided by International Payments, Inc., a Money Service Business (MSB) register with Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN).

A money services business is a legal term used by financial regulators to describe businesses that transmit or convert money.

MSB must comply with the BSA requirements applicable to financial institutions as well as to each of the specific requirements applicable to MSBs.

International Payments will operate only with Local Money Transmitters (LMTs) that are licensed in their countries (or that do not need a license according to their local laws) and with wallet providers in the U.S. that holds the funds in stable coins such as USDC.

Learn more about MSB in the FinCEN webpage www.fincen.gov
