Easy funding/withdrawal

Use your credit/debit card, make a local wire or on-chain from/to a crypto exchange or wallet account.

Deposits

  • Local wires
    Send funds to your Investore account by making a local wire transfer without paying the bank commissions for making international wires. You will only pay a fraction of the cost. See the list of supported countries.
  • Credit and debit cards
    Use your credit or debit card to easily send funds to your Investore account from more than 100 countries. See the list of supported countries.
  • Crypto Exchanges & Wallets
    Send funds to your Investore account from crypto exchanges or digital wallets that trade or are compatible with USDC. See the list of supported exchanges and wallets.
  • Receive from other Investore accounts
    Request money from your friends or family around the world, for free, from any Investore account.

Withdrawals

  • Wires
    Retrieve funds from your account by receiving them in your local bank account. See the list of supported countries.
  • Pay to other Investore accounts
    Send money around the world to your friends or family, for free, to any Investore account.
  • Crypto Exchanges & Wallets
    Fund your Investore account from crypto exchanges or digital wallets that trade or are compatible with USDC. See the list of supported exchanges and wallets.
